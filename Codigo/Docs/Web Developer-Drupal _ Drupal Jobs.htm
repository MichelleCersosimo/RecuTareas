Web Developer-Drupal

The Emmes Corporation

Rockville/MD/US

Share this posting:

  • Novice/Beginner

  • Other

How to apply: 

For immediate consideration please submit your resume and apply directly through the company website at www.emmes.com.

Be sure to mention you found this job on the Drupal Job Board.

Position Details: 

The EMMES Corporation, organized in 1977, is a privately owned Contract Research Organization (CRO) located in Rockville, Maryland. EMMES is dedicated to providing statistical and epidemiological expertise, computer systems development, data management, study monitoring, regulatory guidance, and overall operational support to clients engaged in clinical and biomedical research.

EMMES is seeking a Web Developer to provide support for a custom Drupal platform hosted on-site. Position Summary Support day-to-day on-site operations of a Drupal-based CMS platform including requirements collection, module development, testing, administrative support, training and documentation. Demonstrated experience with the administration of Drupal based systems is preferred, including experience with custom module development. The ideal applicant would also assist with support and administration of Linux based servers for both on-site and cloud-hosted virtual environments. Position will be exposed to new technologies and will be expected to quickly adapt to accommodate changing needs.

Primary Responsibilities

•Support day-to-day operations of Drupal module development, site administration and maintenance

•Support day-to-day administration of on-site and cloud-hosted Linux servers

•Provide timely and professional support for users in a research environment

•Configure new and existing Drupal Sites using core, contributed and custom modules

•Develop custom and maintain custom modules to integrate existing systems and services

•Test and document core and module updates in sandbox and production environments

•Provide training and guidance to project web administrators

•Migrate content and custom modules from Drupal 6 to Drupal 7

•Ability to work independently and as a team member with excellent written and oral communication skills

 

Experience, Competencies and Education

•Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related technical field, or the equivalent of education, certification, training, or work experience

•Minimum of 1 year of experience administering Drupal including custom module development

•Demonstrated experience administering LAMP environments

•Ability to interact with all types of customers and users and provide timely and professional support

•Ability and motivation to excel in a diverse technology driven environment

 

EMMES has an outstanding benefits package including: generous tuition reimbursement, professional development and training programs.

EMMES is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer. EOE/M/F/D/V
